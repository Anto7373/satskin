Skin Tightening

Are you looking for a treatment that will enhance your youthful appearance without any pain, injections, surgery or even any down time?

By providing accurate dermal heating using very focused energy, RF treatments painlessly begin to smooth and tighten your skin immediately. Firmer skin can be after the first treatment.

RF treatments have been clinically proven on all skin types to effectively regenerate collagen, tighten, tone and contour the skin for an overall younger looking appearance. RF treatments are ideal alternative to surgery that can rid your skin of the continual signs of aging.

Who will benefit from RF Treatments?

Seeing as there is no way to completely stop the effects of time and gravity on the skin, RF treatments provides an active solution in the fight against aging. A painless, non- invasive treatment with immediate measurable results for a variety of conditions; firm flabby or lax skin from pregnancy or liposuction, redefine and contour face and neck areas, smooth wrinkles and fine lines.

Since RF treatments do not utilize any type of light and the treatment consists of only heating the tissues under the skin, women of any age or skin type are potential candidates.

How does it work?

RF energy is delivered simultaneously into the deep and shallow layers of the skin all the while protecting the surface layer. This remarkable treatment deals with the root of the problem and not just the symptoms. Collagen is heated and stimulated, creating an immediate contraction, followed by remodeling of the deep skin layers by promoting new collagen growth, which in turn, over a full treatment course, produces tighter, more youthful skin.

How many treatments will I need?

A typical treatments course consists of 6-8 sessions. Each session takes approximately 20 minutes (depending on the area being treated). The option of maintenance treatments(once every 4-8 weeks) is available to client seeking prolonged, enhanced results.

Will it hurt?

Since RF focuses the energy right where it's needed less power in necessary, rendering the treatment virtually painless. During treatments, a mild heating sensation can be felt. Immediately after treatment, some redness and warmth can be seen felt on the skin. Both should disappear within an hour or so following the treatment.

When can I expect to see results?

Immediate results are seen in almost all patients during the first treatments. Further , the desired outcome can be seen over a full treatments course; noticeable reduction in fine lines and wrinkles, face and neck contouring and lifting, and clients report a measurable smaller torso with improved tone and texture of arms and tummy.

Will treatment interrupt my daily routine?

RF treatments are so gentle that you can return to work or your daily routine immediately after completion of your session. This is a simple walk –in, walk-out procedure with no downtime.

Has it been clinically tested?

RF treatments have been clinically proven to tighten and re-contour the skin on the face, neck, arms, and torso, leaving skin looking firmer and younger. Anyone treated with RF can expect collagen contraction, with results varying from person to person; some clients attain a circumference reduction of up to 2cm in the upper arm area. Clients have found treatments to be safe, gentle and effective.
