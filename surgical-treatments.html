About Surgical Treatments

Plastic Surgery today has evolved into several subspecialties that deal with different aspects like Burns surgery, Hand surgery, Craniofacial and Cleft surgery, Head & neck reconstruction and Cosmetic or Aesthetic surgery, to name a few. The main objective in reconstructive surgery is to restore the form and function in other words, to give back the normal appearance and normal function which are absent or lost from various causes.The thrust of aesthetic surgery is to make the appearance more pleasing, achieve refinement in features and restore a more youthful look, whether it is the face, arms, tummy [abdomen] or legs. Towards this end, plastic surgeons use a variety of surgical and non surgical techniques
